CHICKEN RARA


Restaurant style punjabi chicken curry

Chicken rara is a part of punjabi's cousin.This dish very rich and creamy,and flavourable recipe with a huge amount of locan ingrediants that creat an incredible fragrance that can make your mouth water.

Nowadays this dish is cooked in various indian restaurants all over the world.This dish goes really well with rice,bread,and roties

Try this Amazing dish today and if you want the besh dish be sure to buy the fresh chicken and prepare with you own hands



Ingredients
QUANTITY RAW MATERIALS
1 kg Chicken
400 g Chicken Keema
3 qts Chopped Onion
3 qts Tomato
125 ml Curd
60 g Cream
100 g Clarified Butter
6 qts Green Chilli
1 tbsp Salt
2 tbsp Lemon Water
2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
2 tbsp Kashmiri Red Chilli
2 tbsp Red Chilli Powder
2 tbsp Coriander Powder
1/2 tbsp Turmeric Powder
1/2 tbsp Cumin
6 qts Green Cardamom
8 qts Cloves
10 qts Black pepper
2 qts Black Cardamom
1 tbsp Nutmeg
2 sticks Cinnamon Stick
2 qts Nirav Mace
2 qts Bay Leaf
5 qts Kashmiri Red Chilli
20 g Coriander Leaves(to garnish)

Instructions

To make the spice Powder-
  • STEP 1

    • place a non-stick wok on the stove over medium heat.

  • STEP 2

    • Add the cummin seeds,cardamom(black & green),cloves,black,peppercorns,Cinnamon stick,nirav mace,stone flower(chabila),bay leaf,and kashmiri whole red chilies

  • STEP 3

    • Dry toast the spices for 2 minutes by stirring frequently.

  • STEP 4

    • Transfer to a bowl.Once cooled,grind into powder.Keep aside.

    Cooking Process-
  • STEP 5

    • In the same wok,heat the clarified butter over medium-high heat.

  • STEP 6

    • Add the chopped onions and whole green chilies! Stir fry for a few minutes over medium heat until softened and slightly pinkish

  • STEP 7

    • Now,add the chopped tomatoes,salt,red chili powder,coriander powder,kashmiri chili powder,Turmeric powder,& Lemon juice.Mix well for 1-2 minutes.

  • STEP 8

    • Next,pour some water(0.5 cups) and add the chicken pieces.Stir for 2 minutes until the chicken pieces nicely coated with the gravy.Cover the wok for 10 minutes over low heat.

  • STEP 9

    • At this stage,the chicken should reach 70% tenderness.Add the chicken keema(minced meat) and mix nicely.

  • STEP 10

    • Time to add the spice powder(prepared in STEP 1) and ginger-garlic paste.Stir cook for a few minutes over high heat.

  • STEP 11

    • Add the curd and fresh cream.Continue to stir cook the chicken for 5-6 minutes until it releases clarified butter at the edges of the wok.

  • STEP 12

    • Sprinkle fresh coriander Leaves on top,pour water(0.5 cups), and mix gently.Cover the wok for 10-12 minutes over low heat.

  • STEP 13

    • Lastly,uncover the wok,turn off the stove,and serve.
